## The stale cookie error means your browser has corrupted Supabase cookies.

### Quick Fix (Chrome/Edge/Brave):

1. **Open Developer Tools** (F12)
2. Go to **Application** tab (or **Storage** in Firefox)
3. On the left, expand **Cookies**
4. Click on `http://localhost:3000`
5. Look for cookies starting with `sb-`
6. **Right-click each one ‚Üí Delete**
7. **Refresh the page** (F5)

### OR Just Clear All Site Data:

1. Press **F12** (Dev Tools)
2. Go to **Application** ‚Üí **Storage** (left side)
3. Click **"Clear site data"** button
4. Refresh

---

After clearing cookies, try logging in again!
